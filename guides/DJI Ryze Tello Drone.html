The DJI Ryze Tello Drone

The DJI/Ryze Tello is a small, lightweight quadcopter drone built for educational use.

The drone is controlled either through a remote controller/phone app or programmable through its wifi connection and a language like Python or Scratch.

Important

Whilst these drones are small, you should still observe drone safety.


Requirements

1. Laptop or Computer with Wifi capability.
2. Python installed
3. Integrated development environment (IDE)
4. djitellopy Python library installed

Thonny is the IDE used in this guide.

The drone has a small capacity battery. We should keep an eye on it's level as some movements and instructions require a good amount of battery (50% +).
