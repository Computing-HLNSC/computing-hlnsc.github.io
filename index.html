About Us

Herefordshire College is known for providing high-quality IT and computing courses that are practical and help both current and future IT professionals develop the essential skills needed for successful careers in the computing field.

We offer a range of courses, including short fundamental hobby courses up to stage 2 of higher education such as the Higher National Diploma (Level 5), as well as professional courses. Our courses are for those starting out in their careers or those looking to reskill into the IT industry; we provide full-time, short-term, specialised, and hobbyist courses.

Our purpose-built labs, equipped with industry-standard technology, ensure learners gain hands-on experience in computing, networking, cybersecurity, and more.
